Here are some hints to help you succeed at Monkey Run!:
1. Remember, your goal is not only to collect coins,
but to also survive the obstacles. Be careful!
2. Make sure to aways have your hand placed on the
space bar! Obstacles pop up when you least expect it!
3. Don't forget to collect as many coins as possible
to beat your record!
